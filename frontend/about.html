About
What Is HealthGuard India?

HealthGuard India is a free preventive health platform built specifically for Indian users. It combines health risk assessment, blood report reading, personalized Indian diet plans, family health tracking, and health tips in Hindi and English — all in one place.

This tool is not a replacement for a doctor. It is for health awareness so you can have more informed conversations with your healthcare provider.

Scoring
How Is Risk Calculated?
Age
Above 45 years = 2 points
BMI
Overweight / Obese = max 3 points
Blood Pressure
High BP = max 3 points
Blood Sugar
High glucose = max 4 points
Family History
Diabetes / BP in family = 2 points
Activity + Smoking
Sedentary / Smoker = max 4 points
Total 18 points possible. Converted to percentage for risk score. Health Score = 100 - Risk%.
0-30% = Low Risk 31-60% = Moderate 61%+ = High Risk
Medical Disclaimer: HealthGuard India is NOT a medical device. It does not diagnose any disease. All results are estimates. Always consult a qualified doctor.
